WER 2024

WER is an important forum for discussion of relevant issues in the area of Requirements Engineering. The workshop has been held annually since 1998, alternating among Latin American countries, Canada, Spain, and Portugal.

WER is open to the submission of unpublished papers on research, teaching, and industry experience in the field of Requirements Engineering. Although the workshop is based on scientific papers, it provides a variety of tracks, activities, and presentations, as well as opportunities for valuable interactions and networking among participants. WER proceedings are DBLP-indexed and published under ISBN. Additionally, papers can be accessed at the WERpapers repository.

In 2024, in its twenty-seventh edition, WER will be held in the City of Buenos Aires, Argentina.

For more information, contact organizacao.wer@gmail.com.

City of Buenos Aires

The Autonomos City of Buenos Aires is Argentina’s capital city. It offers a packed programme of performing arts; unique neighbourhoods to stroll through during the long days, and exquisite local gastronomy and cocktail bars to enjoy during the night; spaces to share among friends, and spaces to meet new ones.

Its architecture blends classical and modern building styles. At the heart of the city, Plaza de Mayo is surrounded by important nineteenth-century buldings, including Casa Rosada, the iconic presidential home. Must-see attractions include Teatro Colón, a majestic opera house founded in 1908, and MALBA Museum of Fine Arts, which houses one of the best collections of Latin American art. A city for everyone, from sport enthusiasts to art lovers.
