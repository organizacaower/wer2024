Recommendations

  • Don't forget to bring chargers and adapters for your cell phone and/or laptop.
  • Devices can be charged at the cafeteria on the 11th floor and also in the staff rooms on the 6th and 16th floors.
  • The University cafeteria is located on the 11th floor. There is also a café in the entrance to the first basement (Café Havanna).
  • There are lounges on the 6th and 16th floors.
